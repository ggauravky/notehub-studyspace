About NotesHub

Welcome to NotesHub — a platform built by Nikhil , Gaurav and Devansh with the vision of simplifying how students access and organize study material. We are building a powerful, smart, and advanced system that helps you focus on learning instead of searching.

NotesHub is your all-in-one destination for semester notes, important documents, and even engineering books. Whether you want to read online, download, or request specific notes — NotesHub makes it easy and organized.

Our Services

📘 Read & Download Notes

Access all semester notes in structured format. Read directly or download for offline study.

📝 Request Notes

Didn’t find your required notes? You can request them and we’ll add them soon!

📚 Rent or Buy Books

Get engineering books and reference materials — rent them or buy at affordable prices.

☁️ Cloud Storage

Store your notes safely and access them anytime, anywhere — across all your devices.

🚀 Upcoming Features

We’re working on smart features like AI-based note suggestions and a shared study workspace. Stay tuned!
