About NotesHub

NotesHub is a project developed by Nikhil and Gaurav — a platform that helps students organize and access all their semester notes, important documents, and engineering books easily. Our goal is to create a one-stop solution for all academic needs — from downloading notes to renting books and more.

About NotesHub

Welcome to NotesHub — a platform built by Nikhil , Gaurav and Devansh with the vision of simplifying how students access and organize study material. We are building a powerful, smart, and advanced system that helps you focus on learning instead of searching.

NotesHub is your all-in-one destination for semester notes, important documents, and even engineering books. Whether you want to read online, download, or request specific notes — NotesHub makes it easy and organized.
